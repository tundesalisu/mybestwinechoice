Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a wine label?

Simply open the app, tap the camera icon, and point your camera at any wine label. The app will automatically detect and scan the label to provide you with detailed information.

How do I get personalized recommendations?

After scanning a few wines or rating wines you've tried, our app learns your preferences and provides tailored recommendations based on your taste profile.

Can I save my favorite wines?

Yes! Tap the heart icon on any wine to add it to your favorites collection. You can access your saved wines anytime from your profile.

How do I find wine shops near me?

Tap the map icon to view nearby wine shops and restaurants. Make sure location services are enabled for the best experience.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! We take your privacy seriously. Your personal information and wine preferences are stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent.
