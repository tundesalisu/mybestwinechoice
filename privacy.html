Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to The Best Wine Choice ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using The Best Wine Choice app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To personalize your experience and create your user profile
  • Email Address: For account creation, authentication, and communication
  • User ID: To identify and authenticate your account

Usage Information

  • Search History: Wine searches and queries to improve recommendations
  • Product Interactions: How you interact with the app, including wines viewed, saved, and rated
  • Device Information: Device ID, operating system, and app version for analytics and troubleshooting

Location Information

  • Coarse Location: Approximate location to help you find nearby wine shops and restaurants

Photos and Videos

  • Wine Label Photos: Images captured through the app's scanning feature to identify wines and provide information

Technical Data

  • Crash Data: Information about app crashes to improve stability
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics including launch time and responsiveness

How We Use Your Information

App Functionality

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Process wine label scans and provide wine information
  • Display nearby wine locations on the map
  • Save your favorite wines and preferences
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

Personalization

  • Provide personalized wine recommendations based on your preferences
  • Customize your app experience
  • Remember your search history and favorites

Analytics

  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve features
  • Measure app performance and identify areas for improvement

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app, conducting our business, or serving our users
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred
Important: We do not use your data for tracking or targeted advertising purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@mbas-concepts.com

The Best Wine Choice is intended for users who are of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the legal drinking age. If you believe we have collected information from someone underage, please contact us immediately.

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. By using our app, you consent to such transfers.
